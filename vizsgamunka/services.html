Machine manicure

Machine manicure service for both women and men Recommend.

During mechanical manicure service, I push up the skin stuck to the nail, Using a grinding machine, I remove from the surface of the nail the more stubborn skin, then I cut off excess pieces of dead skin with scissors. The nails the length agreed in advance at the start of the manicure service, and form. The surface of the nail can be gently applied according to your wishes I polish it to remove unevenness. The surface of your nails You can ask for it matte or polished to gloss. To complete the service I lubricate the skin area with skin care oil. Service time maximum 30 minutes.

To keep your nails always beautiful and well-groomed, machine manicure service is recommended every 2 weeks.

Service price: 3.500 HUF
